Transfer Technology

DTF

Direct-to-Film Printing

Designs are printed onto a clear film, coated with hot-melt powder, then heat-pressed onto any fabric. Exceptional versatility — works on cotton, polyester, nylon, leather, and dark fabrics alike.

How DTF Works Design is printed on PET film, then coated with hot-melt adhesive powder. Heat press at 160°C bonds the transfer to fabric. The film is peeled away, leaving a flexible, full-color print.

Classic Method

SCREEN

Screen / Silkscreen Printing

Ink forced through a mesh stencil directly onto fabric. Each color requires a separate screen. Produces vivid, ultra-opaque results with excellent durability — the industry standard for bulk production.
